How to Play

Follow the sequence of colors and sounds to win!

  1. Press any key to start the game.
  2. Watch the sequence of colors and sounds carefully.
  3. Repeat the sequence by clicking the buttons (Q for green, W for red, A for yellow, S for blue) or pressing the corresponding keys.
  4. If you get the sequence right, you will move on to the next level with a longer sequence.
  5. If you get the sequence wrong, the game will be over and you can try again.

Good luck and have fun playing!
